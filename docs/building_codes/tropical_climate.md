# 🌴 Tropical Climate Architecture Rules

Designed for hot, humid, high-rainfall environments.

---

## 🌬️ 1. Cooling Strategy
- Prioritize cross-ventilation over mechanical cooling
- Use elevated roofs to reduce heat transfer
- Incorporate shaded courtyards and open corridors

---

## 🌧️ 2. Rain Protection
- Steep roof angles for heavy rainfall runoff
- Extended roof overhangs to protect walls
- Elevated foundations in flood-prone areas

---

## 🌿 3. Material Selection
- Use moisture-resistant materials (treated wood, concrete, stone)
- Avoid materials prone to mold retention
- Promote breathable wall systems

---

## ☀️ 4. Sun Control
- South/North orientation optimization depending on hemisphere
- Use louvers, brise-soleil, and shading screens
- Minimize direct west-facing glass exposure

---

## 🐜 5. Environmental Resilience
- Pest-resistant design (sealed gaps, raised structures)
- Drainage systems to prevent stagnant water
- Vegetation buffers for cooling and humidity control
