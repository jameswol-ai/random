# 🔥 Fire Safety Architecture Rules

Critical safety framework for all building types.

---

## 🚪 1. Evacuation Design
- Minimum 2 exits per floor (or more based on occupancy)
- Exit routes must be clearly visible and unobstructed
- Emergency lighting required along all escape paths

---

## 🧯 2. Fire Suppression
- Fire extinguishers placed at regular intervals
- Sprinkler systems required in:
  - Commercial buildings
  - High-density residential areas
- Fire hydrant access must be ensured externally

---

## 🧱 3. Material Safety
- Avoid highly flammable construction materials
- Fire-rated walls required between compartments
- Electrical systems must include overload protection

---

## 🚨 4. Detection Systems
- Smoke detectors required in all enclosed spaces
- Alarm systems must be audible across entire structure
- Backup power for emergency systems required

---

## 🧭 5. Evacuation Behavior Design
- Clear signage in all directions of movement
- Panic-proof layout (avoid dead-end corridors)
- Assembly points outside building perimeter
