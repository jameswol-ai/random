# 🏗️ Architecture Notes

These notes capture design thinking and evolution of the system.

---

## 🌱 1. Philosophy

Architecture is treated as a **living computation problem**, not static drawing generation.

The system mimics:
- Human design thinking
- Regulatory constraints
- Environmental adaptation

---

## 🧠 2. Key Insight

Instead of generating a final design directly, the system:
- Breaks thinking into stages
- Applies constraints progressively
- Refines output through transformation layers

---

## 🔀 3. Workflow Nature

Workflows behave like:
- Decision trees (simple version)
- Neural pipelines (advanced vision)
- Rule-based reasoning chains

---

## 🌍 4. Environmental Awareness

Design is influenced by:
- Climate conditions
- Local regulations
- Material availability
- Safety constraints

---

## 🧩 5. Future Vision

The system evolves toward:
- Adaptive architectural intelligence
- Region-aware design generation
- Self-optimizing workflows
- Multi-agent architectural reasoning system

---

## 🪶 6. Design Ethos

“Every building is not just constructed — it is negotiated between environment, rules, and intent.”
